Games Keeper

App Description

Games Keeper lets you track scores, no matter what game you play.
Perfect. This app is amazing. ★★★★★
The only scoring app I’ll use. ★★★★★

From tabletop board games to role-playing games, and even outdoor sports. Games Keeper can track any number of players, with different game options (starting scores, hidden scores, lowest score wins, big and small points). It offers quick access to a timer, a dictionary, and dice roll. And you can track games on the go with the stand-alone Apple Watch app.

Visualize the scores with ranks and charts. You can also browse your full games history, with players and scores details, and export them as CSV (requires in-app purchase).

Download Games Keeper for free on the App Store and Mac App Store.
